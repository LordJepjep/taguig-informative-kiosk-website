Steps

Submit duly accomplished application forms, plans and documents indicated on the checklist and get claim stub
Approach the receiving section for your desired transaction with the office and present their documents
.Pay Processing Fee and secure official receipt
Submit all the requirements to any receiving section staff together with the processing fee receipt.
