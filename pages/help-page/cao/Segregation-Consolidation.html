Citizen's Charter

Segregation/Consolidation of Lot (For Titled and Untitled Property)

Where:

  • Office of the City Assessor

Who may avail:

  • Property owner or Authorized representative

Requirements

  • Consolidation/Subdivision plan (for Lot)
  • Updated Tax Clearance
  • Copy of tax declaration
  • Processing Fee
  • For consolidation *Approved Plan
  • Photocopy of document of disposition of property like deed of sale /donation/exchange/Contract to Manage / Assignment / extrajudicial settlement or any other document that were used to dispose the property
  • BIR CAR
  • Transfer Tax Receipt
  • Affidavit of Publication (for extrajudicial settlement/Self-adjudication)
  • for titled property (TCT is required)
  • Notes:

    The above stated turnaround time is the normal processing period, but would still vary depending upon the volume of transactions received by this office.

    The periods of January, March, June, September and December are considered peak seasons for processing of Business Permits and License and payment of Real Property Tax. During this period the office usually experience surge of inquiries and transactions that causes delay in the transaction.

    To process ordinary requests and to minimize possibility of long queue, the office recommend that transactions involving more than 10 properties be considered as bulk transaction requests that will be scheduled for a specific date for release.

    If requestor wants to keep the original copy of certified true copy, please submit a clear and readable photocopy together with the Certified True Copy of the document/s and the Receiving Personnel will return the original copy upon release of the request/s.

    If transfer is included in the transaction, please add the processing time for transfer In case of subdivision of land, please follow process of Appraisal and Assessment of Real Property
