Citizen's Charter

Issuance of Certified True Copies of Tax Declaration/Certification

Where:

  • Office of the City Assessor

Who may avail:

  • Property owner or Authorized representative

Requirements

  • Certified True copy of Tax Declaration
  • Updated tax clearance/receipt (Photocopy)
  • Name and address with valid ID of the person requesting (Photocopy)
  • In case of request for deceased person spa or authorization of the heirs (extrajudicial to prove that the authorizing person is an heir)
  • Tax declaration (Photocopy)
  • Sketch of the property location
  • . Contact number of the owner/representative
  • Updated tax clearance/receipt (Photocopy)
  • Updated tax receipt (Photocopy)
  • Valid ID of the registered owner (Photocopy)
  • Tabulation of Units (Photocopy)
  • Letter Request from the Project owner
Notes:
  • Considering the turnaround time, the office can accommodate around 40 transactions per day, for request of certified true copy and property or no property holdings, this is for transactions that does not require any research. The periods of January, March, June, September and December are considered peak season for processing of Business Permits and License and payment of Real Property Tax.

    During this period the office usually experience surge of inquiries and transactions. This may cause possible delay in the transaction.

    To accommodate ordinary requests and to minimize possibility of long queue, the office recommend that transactions involving more than 10 properties be considered as bulk transaction requests that will be scheduled for a specific date for release If requestor wants to keep the original copy of certified true copy, please submit a clear and readable photocopy together with the Certified True Copy of the document/s and the Receiving Officer will return the original copy upon release of the request/s. Rest assured, considering this is a simple transaction, a maximum period of three (3) business days will still be observed

Steps

Submit documentary requirements
Return to the Office of the City Assessor 2 working days after submission of request, if request requires trace back and receiving of the Order of Payment
Payment
Receiving of Certified True Copy
