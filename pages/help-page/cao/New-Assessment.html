Citizen's Charter

New Assessment/Discovery:Improvements/Machineries

Where:

  • Office of the City Assessor

Who may avail:

  • Property owner or Authorized representative

Requirements

  • Certificate of Occupancy (Photocopy)
  • Tabulation of Saleable & Non-Saleable Area (Photocopy)
  • Soft Copy of Architectural Plan / Autocad Format
  • As Built Plan (Photocopy)
  • . List of Capital Equipment’s w/ acquisition & installation cost /Supply Contract Agreement / P.O. (Photocopy)
  • Lot Title and tax declaration (Photocopy)
  • Processing Fee (Photocopy)
  • Lot - RPT receipt/Tax Clearance (Photocopy)
  • CCT (Photocopy)
  • Barangay Clearance for House Assessment (Photocopy)
  • Affidavit of House Ownership (Photocopy)
  • Building Permit / Certificate of Occupancy / BIF (Photocopy)
  • External Picture of the Structure(Photocopy)
  • Title (Optional) (Photocopy)
  • Tax Declaration of Lot (Photocopy)
  • Technical Description/Lot Data Computation (Photocopy)
  • Location of Property sketch
  • Processing Fee (Photocopy)
  • For Improvement under the Land of Association. Certification from HOA (Photocopy)
  • Sworn Statement (Photocopy)
  • Certificate of Occupancy (Photocopy)
  • Contract of Lease (Photocopy)
  • List of Capital Equipment’s w/ Acquisition cost (Photocopy)
    • Special Power of Attorney or Authorization Letter or Secretary Certificate, in case of corporation (signature of the person authorizing must be the same in the ID of person authorizing)
  • Notes:

    The above stated turnaround time is the normal processing period, but would still vary depending upon the volume of transactions received by this office.

    The periods of January, March, June, September and December are considered peak seasons for processing of Business Permits and License and payment of Real Property Tax. During this period the office usually experience surge of inquiries and transactions that causes delay in the transaction.

    To process ordinary requests and to minimize possibility of long queue, the office recommend that transactions involving more than properties be considered as bulk transaction requests that will be scheduled for a specific date for release.

    If requestor wants to keep the original copy of certified true copy, please submit a clear and readable photocopy together with the Certified True Copy of the document/s and the Receiving Personnel will return the original copy upon release of the request/s.

Steps

Approach the receiving section for your desired transaction with the office and present their documents
Pay Processing Fee and secure official receipt
Submit all the requirements to any receiving section staff together with the processing fee receipt.
Receiving of claim stub issued by the Assessor’s Office-Receiving Section
Receiving of Tax Declaration
