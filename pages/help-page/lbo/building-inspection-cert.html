Citizen's Charter

Issuance of Mechanical Permit
or Certificate of Operation

Where:

  • Local Building Office or Office of the Building Official (City Hall)

Requirements

Three (3) Copies of Mechanical Permit Form
Three (3) Sets of Approved Mechanical Plans
Three (3) Sets of Mechanical Specifications
Three (3) Sets of Cost Estimates
Three (3) Sets of Bill of Materials
Photocopy of valid PRC ID and PTR of a duly licensedProfessional Mechanical Engineer
Notes:

All forms must be fully accomplished and notarized. All documents and plans must be signed and sealed by a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer

  • Three (3) Copies of Certificate of Completion of Mechanical
  • Three (3) Sets of As-built Mechanical Plans
  • Photocopy of Approved Mechanical Permit
  • Inspection and Testing Certificate of the equipment conducted either by the contractor or the third party (must include test results, findings, and recommendations, and signed by those who conduct the inspection and testing)
  • Certification form a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer that the equipment is in good running condition and safe to use
  • Photocopy of valid PRC ID and PTR of a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer
  • Affidavit of Undertaking


  • Notes:

    All forms must be fully accomplished and notarized. All documents and plans must be signed and sealed by a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer

  • Photocopy of Approved Mechanical Permit
  • Photocopy of Approved Mechanical Permit to Operate
  • Photocopy of Approved Mechanical Permit
  • Inspection and Testing Certificate of the equipment conducted either by the contractor or the third party (must include test results, findings, and recommendations, and signed by those who conduct the inspection and testing)
  • Certification form a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer that the equipment is in good running condition and safe to use
  • Photocopy of valid PRC ID and PTR of a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer
  • Affidavit of Undertaking


  • Notes:

    All affidavits must be notarized. All documents must be signed and sealed by a duly licensed Professional Mechanical Engineer

Steps

Submit duly accomplished application forms, plans and documents indicated on the checklist and get claim stub
Return on the date indicated in the stub and inquire on the status of the application in the OBO (LBO). If the application is ready for payment, pay the required fees; otherwise if the application is for compliance, get the documents, comply with the requirements, and go back to step 1.
Present and submit the Official Receipt (OR) to the OBO (LBO) Staff
Present claim stub, claim permit and sign logbook or other transmittals for acknowledgement
