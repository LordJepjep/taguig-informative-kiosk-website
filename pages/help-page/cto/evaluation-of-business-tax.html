Citizen's Charter

EVALUATION OF BUSINESS TAX

Where:

  • BUSINESS TAXES AND FEES SECTION, CITY TREASURER'S OFFICE (City Hall)

Who may avail:

  • All business owners registered in Taguig.

Requirements

Applicaiton Form (completely filled-out)
Photocopy of latest business permit, with billing statement and official receipts
  • VAT/Percentage Tax Returns (Previous Year)
  • Audited Financial Statements (Previous Year)
  • Sworn Declaration of Gross Sales/Receipts (Previous Year)
  • Annual Income Tax Returns (Previous Year)
Recent Community Tax Certificate (If available)
Lease Contract (if applicable)
Articles of Incorporation and SEC Registration (If applicable)
DTI Registration (If applicable)
  • Sworn statement of receipts/sales; or Breakdown of sales per branch duly certifies by the authorized official of the establishment
  • Latest Permit from other cities reflecting declared sales
  • PEZA - registration certificate
  • BOI/PEZA Certification showing incentives duration and entitlement
  • Copy of LGU remittance (if applicable)
  • CDA Registration
  • Certificate of Good Standing
  • DepEd/CHED Accreditation
Other documents that the evaluator may need to assist in the determination of taxes and fees payable to the City
Notes:
  • Volume of taxpayers or transactions, internet connection, power outage, system glitch or any other technical problems may affect the processing time/days mentioned above.
  • Processing time may not apply from January 1-20 / heavy payment periods.

Steps

Submit all documents for evaluation
Receive Billing Statement and Sign in the System logbook
