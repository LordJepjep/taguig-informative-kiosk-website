Citizen's Charter

COMMUNITY TAX CERTIFICATE

Where:

  • CITY TREASURER'S OFFICE (City Hall)

Who may avail:

  • Every resident of the City of Taguig, eighteen (18) years of age or over, who has been regularly employed on a wage or salary basis, or who is engaged in business or occupation, or who owns real property whose assessed value is One Thousand Pesos (P1,000.00) or more, or who is required by law to file an income tax return shall pay an annual community tax.

Requirements

Request form from the Taxpayer indicating Official Receipt Number and Date of IssuanceGross Compensation Income of Employee or Individual Income Tax Return
Audited Financial Statement / VAT Returns in case of Corporation
Gross Income declared by Single Proprietorship Business
Personal Data Sheet

Steps

Proceed to the City Treasurers Office to fill-out the computerized personal data sheet.
Proceed to the Cashier's Counter for payment and issuance of Official Receipt.
