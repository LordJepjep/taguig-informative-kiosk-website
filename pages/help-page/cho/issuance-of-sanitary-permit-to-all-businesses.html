Citizen's Charter

Issuance of Sanitary Permit to all Businesses, Commercial and Industrial Establishments including Public Schools and Government Hospitals, Goverment Facilities and Government Institutions

Where:

  • Business Permits and Licensing Office (City Hall)

Who may avail:

  • Business, Commercial and Industrial Establishments including households, Public Schools and Government Hospitals in Taguig

Requirements

Endorsement from BPLO
Updated Health Certificate For School Teachers and Canteen Staff, Hospital Dietary Staff and Food Handlers at Government Institution and Facilities

Steps

a.BPLO will submit names and details of establishment (From BPLO)
b. Public schools, government hospitals and institutions will submit their updated Health certificate
