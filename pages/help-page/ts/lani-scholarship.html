Citizen's Charter

LANI Scholarship

Where:

  • Taguig Scholarship Offce (CAYETANO SCI-TECH)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Applicant must be already enrolled.
  • If 18 years old or older, applicant must be an active voter of Taguig or voter in the "Embo" area that they are residing in.
  • At least one of the parents is an active voter Taguig or voter in the "Embo" area that they are residing in.
  • Family must be resident of Taguig or in the "Embo" barangay for at least three (3) years and,
  • Of good moral character both in paper and in actual behavior

Requirements

Applied thru Online Application via Taguig Scholarship Facebook page
AUTHENTICATED / CERTIFIED TRUE COPY
Official Form / Registration Form / Certification of Matriculation for current application's semester and school year (current term for Quarter terms and Trimesters)
ORIGINAL / AUTHENTICATED / CERTIFIED TRUE COPY
Certificate of Grades last semester (with school seal / official signature); (For Trimester and Quarter terms, grades for past two (2) semesters)
  • Junior High School Certificate / Grade 10 Report Card / Certification from Principal of the school the applicant graduated From
    (FOR NEW APPLICANT)
  • Senior High School Diploma / Grade 12 Report Card / Certification from Principal of the school the applicant graduated From
    (FOR NEW APPLICANT)
  • School ID or any Government-issued ID (within the validity period, back-to-back on a single page, bearing the applicant's printed name and signature)
    (FOR NEW APPLICANT)
    • e-Card/UMID Card
    • Driver's License
    • Passport
    • SSS ID
    • Voter's ID
    • BIR (TIN ID)
    • PWD ID
    • Solo Parent ID
    • Barangay ID
    • Postal ID
    • PhilHealth ID
    • Philippine ID (PhilID/ePhilID/National ID)
    • AFP Beneficiary / Dependent's ID
  • Birth Certificate (issued by PSA/NSO/Civil Registry Office)

Steps

Apply thru the online application from the Taguig Scholarship Facebook Page
Wait for the official announcement for the current semester at the Taguig Scholarship Facebook Page
Prepare the necessary documents before the assigned submission of documents
