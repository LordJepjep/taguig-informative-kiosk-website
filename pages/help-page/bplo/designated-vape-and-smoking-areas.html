Citizen's Charter

Designated Smoking and Vaping Areas

Description

  • Ordinance No. 15 Series of 2017, requires establishments seeking to set up a designated smoking area to secure a permit for a Designated Smoking or Vaping Area from the Smoke-Free Task Force.

Where:

  • Business Permits and Licensing Office (City Hall)
  • SM Aura

Requirements

Accomplished Application Form (From BPLO)
Floor Plan
Photo of proposed Designated Smoking and Vaping Area

Steps

Submit the duly accomplished Unified Application Form indicating application for a Designated Smoking & Vaping Area along with the requirements
Receive Order of Payment and proceed to the Cashier to pay. Present proof of payment to BPLO.

Joint Inspection team of the Smoke-Free Task Force represented by: BPLO, City Health Office and City Engineering Office will validate the application and verify the information submitted.

Note:

If disapproved, applicant has 30-working days to comply with the requirements.

Claim Order of Payment and Pay fee
Claim the Certification of Operation
