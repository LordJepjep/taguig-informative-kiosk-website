Citizen's Charter

Securing New Business Permit

Description

  • All enterprises are required to secure a Business Permit before the start of business operations.
  • All issued permits are subject to "Post-Audit" process.
  • Inspection may take 1-2 days depending on the status of the business or if the establishment has no representative present atthe declared office.

Where:

  • Business Permits and Licensing Office (City Hall)
  • SM Aura

Requirements

Accomplished Application Form (From BPLO)
  • Articles of Incorporation
  • DTI Registration
  • CDA Accreditation
  • If renting: Lease Contract
  • If property Owned: Tax declaration
  • If not renting: Letter of Consent
Barangay Clearance
Locational Clearance & Zoning Clearance from (from CPDO)
Occupancy Permit
  • 3R picture of establishment colored
  • Company Logo (forcorporation, Partnership, CDA)
  • 2x2 ID picture for Single Proprietorship
Insurance (CGL)
Official receipt and Statement of Account (from CTO)
Some business may have additional requirements imposed by National Agencies.

    Any of the following:

  • Letter of Authority
  • Special Power of Attorney
  • or Secretary’s Certificate

Steps

Submit the duly accomplished application form with complete requirements
Pay relevant Business Taxes and Fees to the City Treasurers Office
Present the Statement of Account and Official Receipt from the City Treasurer’s Office and claim Business Permit
